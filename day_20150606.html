Saturday, June 6, 2015

Today we set off for Yosemite on a two day camping trip. Here are some nice dry California hills.

On the way to Yosemite, we stopped at Mariposa Grove to see some big trees and do some hiking. Andrew got poison oak.

Here are some big trees. People are shown for scale.

Unfortunately, the Mariposa Grove Museum was closed (permanently). Some passerbys said that it was closed in preparation for a large construction project that the park was going to do for the next three years! They were planning to revamp the entire area of the park. As it turned out, we might have been some of the last people to be allowed into the park for the next three years.

We met a couple of critters along the way...

And we finally arrived at Yosemite that evening...

Behind us is Yosemite Falls...
